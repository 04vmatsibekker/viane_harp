About Viane

Creating beautiful music for the moments that matter most.

Hi, I'm Viane, a harpist based in Central New Jersey with over 10 years of experience creating beautiful music for life's most meaningful moments. Music has been a central part of my life, and I've had the privilege of performing in a wide variety of settings—from elegant weddings and private celebrations to fashion shows, college orchestras, and even Carnegie Hall.

Whether you're planning a wedding, cocktail hour, corporate event, bridal shower, or another special occasion, I'd be honored to provide an elegant and memorable soundtrack tailored to your event. My goal is to create an atmosphere that is both beautiful and unforgettable.
