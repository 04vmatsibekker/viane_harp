About Me

Warm, professional harp music tailored for weddings, receptions, ceremonies, and bespoke events. A calm presence with polished repertoire and thoughtful presentation.

Meet Viane, your harpist for unforgettable celebrations.

With more than a decade of live performance experience, Viane brings graceful harp music to weddings, ceremonies, corporate gatherings, and private receptions. She combines classical technique with modern repertoire to create a timeless atmosphere for every event.

Her mission is to elevate your special day through music that feels elegant, emotional, and beautifully suited to the moment.

  • Years of experience Professional musician since 2014.
  • Musical training Conservatory studies, chamber music, and performance coaching.
  • Performance specialties Weddings, ceremonies, corporate receptions, and private events.
  • Event experience Hotels, gardens, galleries, churches, and exclusive venues.
