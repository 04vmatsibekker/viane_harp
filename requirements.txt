For BSA - maggot brain 

A solo harp performance of Maggot Brain, presented for the RPI Black Student Association Fashion Show. Originally written by Funkadelic, this iconic piece is known for its expressive, guitar-driven melody.
For fleurs 

An original harp composition inspired by the quiet return of spring. Fleurs is a reflective and somber work that captures the resilience of nature as it begins to bloom again, symbolizing renewal, perseverance, and the enduring pursuit of hope after periods of hardship.
for Gitana 

A live harp performance recorded in Arizona. Gitana is a vibrant Spanish-inspired work that combines energetic rhythms, sweeping arpeggios, and lyrical passages.

Canon in D 
One of the most recognizable pieces of classical music, Canon in D by Johann Pachelbel is celebrated for its elegant, flowing melody and timeless beauty. It has become a traditional choice for wedding ceremonies around the world, often accompanying the bridal procession or other significant moments of the celebration. This performance highlights the harp's warm, lyrical sound while honoring the grace and serenity that have made the piece a lasting favorite.
